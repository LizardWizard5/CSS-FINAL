About Xbox

Beginning with 4 Microsoft engineers, Xbox is console and video game brand that began on November 15th 2001 with it's first console the Xbox and was instantly put into competition with other big brands. and is still a leading competitor in the console industry. Xbox is growing with new studios and new games being added all the time.

XBOX

Released November 15th 2001 and was the first console Xbox released, and was the first console to release with Ethernet based internet connection and a fee based online gaming service. The Xbox had a record breaking launch in North America with 1.5 million units sold before the end of the year. It also launched with the exclusive title Halo: Combat Evolved.

XBOX 360

Released on November 22nd 2005, The Xbox 360 is the second console in the Xbox line and was the first console to feature the online service Xbox Live! Xbox Live allowed for people to get games online, stream tv and play games together. after this model, there are 2 more versions of the xbox 360. In 2010 the Slim and in 2013, the Xbox 360 E

XBOX ONE

Released on November 22 2013, The Xbox One is the third console in the Xbox line. It was powered by a AMD CPU and brought more ideas to Cloud Gaming and was the first Xbox console to have a Blu-Ray drive. The console brought a new controller and allowed you to connect your Windows 10 pc and stream your game from your Xbox to your pc. After the Xbox One, two more models released, the One S and the One X

XBOX SERIES X|S

Released on November 10th 2020, the Xbox Series X|S is the newest of the Xbox lineup. Powered by a AMD CPU and GPU the Xbox SERIES X|S brought a big leap in performance with the Xbox SERIES X being able to run 4K with Raytraciing at 120FPS. The Xbox SERIES X|S is almost completely backwards compatible and can run almost all old Xbox games
