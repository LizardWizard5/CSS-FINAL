WHAT IS GAMEPASS?

Gamepass is a monthly subscription with two tiers Ultimate and Basic, Ultimate grants you access to Xbox Gold and Gamepass on Xbox and PC along with the option of cloud gaming, while Basic lets you choose to have Gamepass on Xbox or PC. Gamepass comes with over 100 triple A titles including all 1st party Xbox studio games day 1, it also comes with EA play another game subscription(Ultimate Only), Ubisoft games and other bonuses on platforms like Riot games
